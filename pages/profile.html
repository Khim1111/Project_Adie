Who is Adie?

Adie Garcia (a.k.a. Adie) is a Filipino singer, model, and social media influencer. He became known to the public for his viral Youtube videos where he belts out popular songs. He signed up as an artist of O/C Records of singer Kean Cipriano in 2020. His single "Paraluman" was a big hit with more than 3.5 million views on its first month alone. It also made it to No. 1 in the Viral Top-50 on Spotify. Written and directed by Bela Padilla, the official music video features another social media sensation Ivana Alawi. The song has been one of the biggest songs of 2021 and has been a comfort for Pinoy music fans for its lifting music and Adie’s effervescent singing.

After being scouted and signed by O/C Records, 20-year-old singer and songwriter Adie Garcia had a colorful journey. He has since become a steady source of hit singles for OC Records that include “Tahanan,” “Dungaw” and Janine Berdin featured-hit “Mahika”. He also became one of the occasional performers of ASAP Natin To, the Sunday afternoon show of ABS-CBN media network.

On June 9, 2022, Adie Garcia was chosen as the Best Male Artist at the New Hue Music Awards. Adie said he is nothing but thankful and now has a more hopeful outlook for the coming years.

“Hindi ko alam kung paano ko ipu-put into words yung mga achievements at lahat ng nangyari sa akin this year. Pero sobrang blessed lang talaga siguro sa lahat ng na-achieve ko this year. Napaka-surreal lang sa tulad ko na nag-start lang this pandemic kaya hindi ko talaga ito in-expect ng ganitong kabilis. Di ba usually pag starting artist ka, ang daming pagdadaanan, ang dami mong kailangan na ma-experience na ganito, ganyan. But for me parang ang bilis lang. Hindi ko alam kung meant ba sa akin ito or what, ero ang masasabi ko lang sobrang blessed ko lang talaga siguro na maranasan ito agad agad. With the song ‘Paraluman’ sobrang game changer nung song na yun for me. Ang dami nabago sa life ko simula nung ni-release ko yung song na yun. Sobrang bilis lang talaga. Sobrang grateful ko lang talaga sa lahat ng na-achieve ko this year and sa mga susunod pa,” he said.

Published under O/C Records, Adie’s latest single “G.K.Y.A.M” is now out on all leading digital music platforms, including Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube.
