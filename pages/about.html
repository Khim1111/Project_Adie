Adie Garcia (a.k.a. Adie) is a Filipino singer, model, and social media influencer. He became known to the public for his viral Youtube videos where he belts out popular songs. He signed up as an artist of O/C Records of singer Kean Cipriano in 2020. His single "Paraluman" was a big hit with more than 3.5 million views on its first month alone. It also made it to No. 1 in the Viral Top-50 on Spotify. Written and directed by Bela Padilla, the official music video features another social media sensation Ivana Alawi. The song has been one of the biggest songs of 2021 and has been a comfort for Pinoy music fans for its lifting music and Adie’s effervescent singing.

After being scouted and signed by O/C Records, 20-year-old singer and songwriter Adie Garcia had a colorful journey. He has since become a steady source of hit singles for OC Records that include “Tahanan,” “Dungaw” and Janine Berdin featured-hit “Mahika”. He also became one of the occasional performers of ASAP Natin To, the Sunday afternoon show of ABS-CBN media network.
